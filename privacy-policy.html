Privacy Policy

At Fluency Link Academy, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure its security. By using our website, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

As a trusted provider of English language courses, we aim to maintain transparency and foster trust with our students and users. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and our obligations.

What information do we collect?

Fluency Link Academy collects personal information to deliver high-quality services tailored to your learning goals. We may collect:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number, and location.
  • Payment information, including billing address and transaction details.
  • Learning preferences and progress data from your interactions with our courses.
  • Technical data such as IP address, device type, and browser preferences for improved functionality.

This information allows us to personalize your experience, improve our platform, and deliver effective communication.

How do we use your information?

The data collected is used exclusively to provide you with the best learning experience. Specific uses include:

  • Facilitating course enrollment and access to materials.
  • Offering tailored feedback and progress tracking.
  • Sending updates, newsletters, and information about new courses or promotions.
  • Analyzing user activity to enhance platform performance and course content.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.

How long do we keep your information?

Your personal information is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Transactional data is stored for tax and record-keeping purposes.
  • Course progress data is retained to maintain continuity in your learning.
  • Marketing preferences are honored until you opt out.

Upon request, we will delete or anonymize your personal data, except where required by law.

What are your privacy rights?

As a student or user of Fluency Link Academy, you have the following rights:

  • Access your personal data and request copies.
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your information.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your information under certain circumstances.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us directly through our support channels.

How do we keep your information safe?

Fluency Link Academy prioritizes the security of your data and implements industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission.
  • Secure storage of information on restricted-access servers.
  • Regular updates to security systems to address emerging threats.
  • Employee training on data protection best practices.

While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure. Please notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Fluency Link Academy reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any significant updates will be communicated to users via email or notifications on our platform.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
