Have questions?
We have answers.

Find answers to common questions about payment, course enrollment, and technical details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via UPI, credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile wallets such as Paytm and Google Pay.

Is there an option to pay in installments?

Yes, installment plans are available for select courses. Contact us to discuss payment options and eligibility.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available if requested within the first two weeks of enrollment. Please visit our refund policy page for more details.

Are there any additional fees for the courses?

Our fees are all-inclusive, covering materials and platform access. Additional fees may apply for certification exams.

How can I get a receipt for my payment?

A receipt will be emailed to you after payment confirmation. For hard copies, contact our support team.

Can I switch to a different course after enrollment?

Course changes are allowed within the first week of enrollment. Please contact our team for guidance and availability.
